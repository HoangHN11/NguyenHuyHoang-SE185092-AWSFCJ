### Week 3 Objectives:

* Focus on **Networking in AWS**, specifically **VPC** and **Route 53**.  
* Set up secure networking and domain management in AWS.  
* Learn about **Security Groups**, **NACLs**, and **VPN**.  
* Practice labs for networking services and security configurations.

* Created and configured **VPC** with subnets, route tables, and internet gateways.
