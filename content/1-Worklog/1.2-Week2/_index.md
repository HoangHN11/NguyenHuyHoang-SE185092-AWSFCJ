### Week 2 Objectives:

* Deep dive into **AWS services**, focusing on **Compute**, **Networking**, and **Storage**.  
* Get familiar with **AWS EC2**, **S3**, **Lambda**, and **Elastic Beanstalk**.  
* Practice creating and managing instances, working with storage, and deploying web applications.  
* Understand **AWS IAM** for identity and access management.
